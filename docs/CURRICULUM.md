# Educational Curriculum

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  

## Overview

Educational outreach materials for community engagement in the Lyari Basin river restoration initiative. These materials will cover:

- Basic robotics education for local schools
- Environmental awareness workshops
- Technical training for aspiring operators
- Safety protocols for community volunteers

*Content to be developed as part of the worker welfare training program under "Upward Mobility & Executive Training" budget allocation.*
