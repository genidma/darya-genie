# Module 1: Safety Fundamentals & PPE

**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Required Score:** 100% for completion

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, learners will be able to:
- Identify primary hazards in the Lyari Basin environment
- Demonstrate proper PPE usage and limitations
- Recognize emergency stop procedures and communication signals

## 1.1 Environmental Hazards

### Toxic Sludge Exposure
- **Heavy Metals:** Lead, mercury, and cadmium concentrations exceed WHO limits by 300%
- **Chemical Runoff:** Industrial discharge creates pH levels ranging from 4.2 to 11.8
- **Biological Contamination:** E. coli counts average 24,000 CFU/mL (safe limit: <100 CFU/mL)

### Physical Hazards
- **Unstable Banks:** Soil erosion creates collapse risk within 2 meters of water edge
- **Hidden Debris:** Sharp metal, broken glass, and collapsed structures below waterline
- **Weather Conditions:** Monsoon flash floods can rise water level 2 meters in <30 minutes

## 1.2 Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

| Item | Specification | Inspection Frequency |
|------|---------------|---------------------|
| Respirator | P100 filter, chemical-resistant | Daily fit-check |
| Gloves | Nitrile, 6mm thickness | Replace every 4 hours |
| Boots | Steel-toe, chemical-resistant | Weekly integrity check |
| Coveralls | Tyvek, full-body coverage | Replace after each shift |

## 1.3 Emergency Procedures

**STOP WORK Signal:** Three short whistle blasts or "STOP" call over radio  
**EVACUATE Signal:** Continuous siren for 30 seconds  
**Medical Emergency:** "MEDIC" repeated three times over Channel 1
