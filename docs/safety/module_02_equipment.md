# Module 2: Equipment Handling & Protocols

**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Required Score:** 100% for completion

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, learners will be able to:
- Operate UGV and drone equipment safely within defined parameters
- Follow proper shutdown and storage procedures
- Execute basic troubleshooting for common equipment issues

## 2.1 UGV Operations

### Pre-Operation Checklist
- Battery voltage: 12.6V minimum for LiFePO4 packs
- Track tension: 20mm above ground level
- Conveyor belt: Clear of debris, tension verified
- Sensor integrity: All cameras and lidars unobstructed

### Operational Limits
- **Water Depth:** Maximum 40cm for standard tracks
- **Slope:** Maximum 15° incline to prevent rollover
- **Temperature:** Operational between -10°C to 50°C
- **Load:** Maximum 25kg collection capacity

## 2.2 Drone Operations

### Flight Envelope
- **Wind Speed:** Maximum 15 km/h
- **Altitude:** Maximum 120m AGL (CAA limit)
- **Battery:** Return-to-Home at 30% remaining
- **Obstacle Distance:** Minimum 15m from people/structures

### Pre-Flight Protocol
1. GPS lock verification (minimum 8 satellites)
2. Propeller inspection for cracks or wear
3. Control link signal strength test (> -85 dBm)
4. Vision system calibration check

## 2.3 Post-Operation Procedures

### UGV Shutdown
1. Halt all motors and wait 30 seconds for cooldown
2. Remove battery packs and place in fire-retardant bags
3. Clean tracks and conveyor with fresh water
4. Log operational hours and any anomalies

### Drone Storage
1. Remove batteries immediately after landing
2. Wipe down props and body with damp cloth
3. Store in climate-controlled cases with silica gel
4. Charge batteries to 3.8V storage level
